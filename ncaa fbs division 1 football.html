Heisman Trophy Predictions

One of the highest individual achievements you can win in college football is the Heisman trophy. My top 5 Heisman trophy candidate picks are Dede Westbrook, Lamar Jackson, Donnel Pumphrey, Jalen Hurts, and Deshaun Watson. Dede Westbrook is a starting wide receiver for the Oklahoma Sooners, and this season, he is tied for fourth in most receiving yards this season with 1254 yards and 14 touchdowns, and has caught 68 passes. Donnel Pumphrey is a starting running back for the San Diego State Aztecs, who leads the NCAA in rushing yards this season with 1779. Jalen Hurts is the starting quarterback for the Alabama Crimson Tide, this season, he has thrown 16 touchdowns and ran for 11 touchdowns. Deshaun Watson is the starting quarterback for the Clemson Tigers, during his 3 year NCAA career he has 8647 passing yards with 76 passing touchdowns, and 1683 rushing yards with 19 rushing touchdowns. Lamar Jackson is the starting quarterback for the Louisville Cardinals, this season he has a total of 47 touchdowns with 28 passing and 19 rushing, he is also responsible for 4476 total yards with 3109 passing and 1367 rushing yards. This is why I think Lamar Jackson will win the Heisman trophy in 2016.
